The Veteran Lead Generation Problem

Veterans need your help. But most don't know you exist—and the ones who find you aren't always ready to proceed. You're spending hours on consultation calls with people who:

  • ✗ Aren't actually eligible — still serving, no service-related conditions, or already claimed everything
  • ✗ Are "just looking" — curious but not ready to commit to the claims process
  • ✗ Don't trust advocates — burned by lawyers or confused about fees
  • ✗ Give fake contact details — want information but don't want calls

Every unqualified consultation is an hour you could've spent helping a veteran who's actually ready. Your time is your most valuable asset.

🔴 The Problem: "Contact Us" Forms Don't Work

A generic contact form captures everyone—eligible or not, ready or not, real details or fake. You waste hours qualifying leads who should never have reached you.

Result: 60%+ of consultations go nowhere.

🟢 The Solution: Interactive Eligibility Assessments

Replace your contact form with an interactive "Am I Eligible?" assessment that educates veterans while capturing qualification data.

  • Service History Questions "When did you serve?" "Which branch?" — filters out non-veterans
  • Condition Checklist "Select conditions you experience" — identifies claim potential
  • Entitlement Preview "You may be entitled to $X" — builds trust through value

🔴 The Problem: Veterans Don't Trust Advocates

Many veterans have been burned by lawyers or are suspicious of "claims farmers." They want help but won't give their details to a stranger.

Result: High-intent veterans bounce without converting.

🟢 The Solution: Value-First Lead Capture

Give veterans something valuable—a personalised entitlement estimate—before asking for contact details. They experience your expertise before committing.

  • Personalised Estimates "Based on your conditions, you may be entitled to $80,000-$120,000"
  • Education Content Explain DRCA vs MRCA, what PI means, Gold Card eligibility
  • Ex-DVA Positioning Show your credentials at the moment of highest trust

🔴 The Problem: Fake Phone Numbers

Veterans want the information but don't want sales calls. They submit fake numbers. You call, no answer, wasted time.

Result: 30-40% of leads are unreachable.

🟢 The Solution: SMS Verification

Verify phone numbers before they reach your inbox. Position it as "We'll text your personalised report"—they're receiving value, not being checked.

  • SMS Code Verification Real number confirmed before you see the lead
  • Framed as Delivery "Enter code to receive your entitlement report"
  • 100% Reachable Every lead you call will actually answer

🔴 The Problem: Which Ads Actually Work?

You're running Facebook ads, Google ads, maybe some content marketing. But which campaigns produce clients who actually sign? Which produce tyre-kickers?

Result: Wasting ad spend on campaigns that generate junk leads.

🟢 The Solution: Full Attribution Tracking

Track every lead from ad click to signed client. Know which campaigns, headlines, and audiences produce your best clients.

  • Source Tracking Which ad campaign brought this veteran?
  • Quality by Source Facebook vs Google—which produces better clients?
  • ROI Visibility Know your true cost per signed client, not just cost per lead
